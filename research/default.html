Research Interests

My research interests are Machine Learning and Statistical Pattern Recognition, specifically dimensionality reduction, manifold learning and data fusion from disparate data sources. Please see my CV for my research and teaching experience.

I am also interested in statistical applications  in bioinformatics and computational biology.
