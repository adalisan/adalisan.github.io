Research Interests

My research interests are Machine Learning and Statistical Pattern Recognition, specifically dimensionality reduction, manifold learning and data fusion from disparate data sources. Please see my

I am Sancar Adali, a PhD recipient in Applied Mathematics and Statistics at Johns Hopkins University.
I expect to graduate in May 2013. Here's my resume and link to CV.

My thesis advisor is Professor Carey E. Priebe .

My dissertation is on using data from disparate sources in dissimilarity representation to do joint statistical inference. Here is a good introduction by Zhiliang Ma, a previous Ph.D student and my advisor.
